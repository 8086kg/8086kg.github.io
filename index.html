Enterprise Management

For most people, Apple is just a company. But for me, it was like a university.

From 2012 to 2017, I worked at Apple’s Beijing branch. Starting as a Family Room Specialist and eventually becoming aCreative Pro.

I gained a profound understanding of how Apple embodies the core principles of simple, beautiful, convincing, and empowering in its products and services. I witnessed how Apple values its diverse employees as the most valuable resource, how it upholds business conduct of honesty, respect, confidentiality, and compliance even in the most critical public relations moments, and how it maintains a vibrant culture of innovation as a multinational corporation.

In my last few months at Apple, I joined the Lead and Learn(L&L) program to train myself to become an excellent manager.

After leaving Apple, I joined Gotawolf, a startup cultural media company, as the Creative Director. In this role, I recruited and mentored four recent graduates, helping them enhance their professional skills and training them to become creative specialists.

Computer Programming

I stumbled into the world of computer programming by chance, but this journey has been incredibly exciting for me!

In 2020, while working at Gotawolf we needed an internal system that would allow all clients to log in and write drafts of their articles, which would then be further edited and polished by our editorial team before being published on social media. Additionally, we needed a comprehensive set of tools to analyze and track data within this system.

Faced with two challenges—finding a ready-made solution that perfectly fit all our requirements and having limited funds for custom development—I decided to venture into the mysterious field of programming.

Starting off was extremely challenging, but I applied the exploratory spirit I learned in my college major, utilized the learning methods I acquired from Li Yang Crazy English, leveraged my English proficiency, and applied the project management skills I gained at Apple. After six months of development, testing, and implementation, I not only successfully delivered the project but also fell in love with programming. Since then, I have developedover 30 tools for different departments, addressing various work details and saving labor costs, making everyone’s work easier.

Furthermore, I have helped numerous external clients, partners, and friends by assisting them in building websites or developing customized internal tools. These include creating website prototypes for CVERC, Yuanrun Holdings, and STW Food Company, developing a static website prototype for a news agency along with an internally used generative AI-based editing assistant, building a website that provides transparency in used car prices, setting up my own technology blog, and creating my own AI assistant tool.
